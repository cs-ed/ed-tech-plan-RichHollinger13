--- title: Educational Technology Plan layout: page --- # Educational Technology Plan The main purpose of this Educational Technology Plan is to develop an approach to teaching AP Computer Science A in an effective and engaging way while being aware of the technological challenges that may arise from different software and hardware. Additionally, the plan is developed to maintain equity amongst students and encourage growth. ## Context ### Course This Educational Technology Plan is designed for AP Computer Science A, an advanced placement course targeting students in grades 10 through 12. Instructional units cover Java, OOP, and computational thinking. ### Situation Classroom: San Marino High School, 19 students, Chromebooks + Apple desktops. ## Plan ### Software - VS Code as IDE - GitHub for assignments - Schoology as LMS ### Hardware - Instructor: MacBook Pro 13", 24GB RAM, Apple M4 CPU - Students: M1 Macs + Acer laptops ## Impacts - Inclusion & Accessibility - Resiliency & Contingency - Ethical Considerations
